The Belgian Shepherd Tervuren

Looooong Hair!

The Tervuren is colored very similarly to a Malinois (that sable brown with a dark mask), but it has much longer hair. Some even bear a striking resemblance to Collies!


The Tervuren, like other Dutch Shepherd breeds, is a high-energy dog with a need for lots of exercise. They take a lot of pride in their work and are happy to run the fields all day herding sheep or other livestock. Just as intelligent at the other varieties, they can take cues from across a field to help their owner with ranching and other such tasks.
