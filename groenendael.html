The Belgian Shepherd Groenendael

So Spooky!

Here, we have the Dutch Shepherd Groenendael (pronounced GROW-uh-nun-dale). Like the Tervuren, they have very long hair, but this variety of Dutch Shepherd is black as night! Well, except for the occasional splash of white on the chest. That white patch is actually acceptable in show dogs, believe it or not. The Groenendael isn't much different from the other varieties of Belgian Sheepdog. High-energy, with endless love to give, and a keen intelligence, the Groenedael would probably run the household if it had thumbs.
