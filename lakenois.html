The Belgian Shepherd Laekenois

He'll Poof and He'll Floof!

The Laekenois is probably the variety that sticks out the most! I'd call it the "black sheep" of the family, except it's not black. It has a longer coat than the Malinois, but a shorter coat than either the Tervuren or the Groenendael. That coat, though, is wiry!


Just as energetic as the other Dutch Shepherd varieties, the Laekenois excels in an active lifestyle. They're wonderful companions, too, provided you take them for lots of walkies! As you'll see below, some of them even compete in athletic events.
